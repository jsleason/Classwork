Fundraising

Where does the money go?

Our beneficiaries are C.S. Mott and Beaumont Children's Hospitals. The money we raise helps spread funds and awareness for pediatric rehabilitation programs. $3 for one week of social integration bowling vs $4.25 for a tall Starbucks Pumpkin Spice Latte. $6 for one session of Aquatic Fitness physical therapy vs. $6.25 for a Chipotle Chicken Burrito (without Guac). $17 for one session of theraputic Martial Arts vs. $17 for a jumbo Pizza House feta bread. $21 for one Talk Around Town session for a child who uses speech generating devices to encourage social interraction within his or her community vs. $20 for a Michigan Football Season T-Shirt at the MDen. $52 for one Tree Climbing Therapy session which uses ropes to allow a child to climb a tree and develop new strengths, vs. $50 for an overpriced economics textbook.

Fundraising Ideas

Go Bucketing!

Send emails and hand-written letters to people who may be willing to support your fundraising! THANK YOUR DONORS! Donors are much more likely to donate in the future if they are stewarded. Make sure to ask whoever is donating to you to check if their company participates in corporate matching! If the employer does participate, you could be on the fast track to smashing your fundraising goal! Do odd jobs for friends (cook dinner, clean room, do laundry, deliver food, rake leaves, shovel snow)! Post your DonorDrive link to social media! Collect change in a jar and donate it at the end of the year! Sell baked goods.
